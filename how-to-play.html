How to Play Tetrix

Tetrix is a relaxing block puzzle game where you drag and drop colorful shapes onto a 10√ó10 grid. Clear complete rows and columns to score points, unlock shape rotation abilities, and keep the puzzle going. There's no time limit‚Äîtake your time, think strategically, and enjoy the satisfying gameplay!

Drag shapes from the Shape Queue

GIF

At the bottom of the screen, you'll see your Shape Queue with three colorful shapes ready to use. To play, simply click and hold any shape to pick it up and start dragging. The shapes come in various Tetris-like configurations‚Äî some are straight lines, L-shapes, squares, T-shapes, and more.

Key controls:

  • Click and hold on any shape to grab it
  • Drag the shape around with your mouse or finger
  • The shape will follow your cursor as you move it over the grid
  • Each shape has a unique color and pattern of blocks

Place them on the Grid

GIF

The game board is a 10√ó10 grid where you'll place your shapes. As you drag a shape over the grid, the game shows you exactly where it will land. Valid placements appear bright and colorful, while invalid spots (overlapping existing blocks or going off the grid) appear faded and semi-transparent.

Placement rules:

  • Valid placement: The shape appears bright when it can be placed
  • Invalid placement: The shape appears faded when it overlaps or goes out of bounds
  • Release to place: Let go of the mouse or lift your finger to drop the shape
  • Undo drag: Drag the shape back to the queue area to cancel placement

Once placed, shapes become permanent blocks on the grid. Plan ahead to maximize your line clearing opportunities!

Fill in Rows and Columns to clear lines

GIF

The core mechanic of Tetrix is line clearing. When you completely fill any row (horizontal) or column (vertical) with blocks, that entire line disappears! This is how you prevent the grid from filling up and create space for more shapes.

Line clearing mechanics:

  • Complete rows: Fill all 10 blocks horizontally across any row to clear it
  • Complete columns: Fill all 10 blocks vertically down any column to clear it
  • Multiple clears: Clear multiple rows and columns with a single shape placement
  • Strategic combos: The more lines you clear at once, the more points you earn
  • Visual feedback: Watch lines highlight and disappear with satisfying animations

Think ahead and try to set up positions where one shape placement can clear multiple lines at once!
